About

The Sultan Ahmad Shah Mosque stands as a magnificent example of Islamic architecture in Pekan. Built in the traditional Malay-Islamic style, this mosque serves as both a religious center and an architectural landmark.

The mosque features stunning geometric patterns, intricate calligraphy, and a grand prayer hall that can accommodate hundreds of worshippers. Its serene atmosphere and beautiful gardens make it a must-visit destination.
