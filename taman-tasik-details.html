About

Taman Tasik Sultan Abu Bakar is a serene lakeside park offering a perfect retreat for nature lovers and families. The park features well-maintained walking paths, exercise stations, and beautiful landscaping around a scenic lake.

Visitors can enjoy various recreational activities, including jogging, picnicking, and bird watching. The park's peaceful atmosphere makes it an ideal spot for morning exercises or evening relaxation.
